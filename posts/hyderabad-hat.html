Hyderabad Hat

HUFA (Hyderabad Ultimate Frisbee Association) is organizing a HAT tournament in Hyderabad on 17th and 18th August (Sat, Sun), at CCB grounds, Near Shamshabad International Airport.

For the uninitialized, a hat tournament is a tournament where players register as individuals and teams are formed by drawing players names out of a hat.

Register here before 24th.
